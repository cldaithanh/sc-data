1.22. Xuc Cham

Khong xuc, khong co cham,
Co xuc, thoi co cham,
Nen hai nguoi khong hai,
Tuc co xuc, co cham,
Ai hai nguoi khong hai,
Nguoi tinh, khong o nhiem,
Ke ngu hai qua ac,
Nhu nguoc gio tung bui.
