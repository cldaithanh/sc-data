22.154. Ta Kien: Micchaditthi

Vi-n 1-3 Nhan duyen o Savatthi…

—Nay cac Ty-kheo, do co cai gi, do chap thu gi, do thien chap gi, ta kien khoi len?

Vi-n 4 —Ðoi voi chung con, bach The Ton, cac phap lay The Ton lam can ban…

Vi-n 5 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, do co sac, co chap thu sac, do thien chap sac nen ta kien khoi len.

Vi-n 6-9 … tho… tuong… cac hanh…

Do co thuc, do co chap thu thuc, do thien chap thuc nen ta kien khoi len.

Vi-n 10 Cac ong nghi the nao, nay cac Ty-kheo, sac la thuong hay vo thuong?

—La vo thuong, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong la kho hay lac?

—La kho, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong, kho, chiu su bien hoai, co nen chap thu cai ay, va khoi len ta kien khong?

—Thua khong, bach The Ton.

Vi-n 11-14 … Tho… Tuong… Cac hanh… Thuc la thuong hay vo thuong?

—La vo thuong, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong la kho hay lac?

—La kho, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong, kho, chiu su bien hoai, co nen chap thu cai ay, va khoi len ta kien khong?

—Thua khong, bach The Ton.

Do thay vay… vi ay biet ro… “… khong con tro lui trang thai nay nua”.
