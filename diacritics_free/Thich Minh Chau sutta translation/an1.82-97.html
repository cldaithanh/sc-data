• Anguttara Nikaya
  • IX. Pham Phong Dat

1.82–97

TTC 1Ta khong thay mot phap nao khac, nay cac Ty-kheo, co the đua đen bat loi lon, nay cac Ty-kheo, nhu la phong dat. Phong dat, nay cac Ty-kheo, đua đen bat loi lon.

TTC 2Ta khong thay mot phap nao khac, nay cac Ty-kheo, co the đua đen ich loi lon, nay cac Ty-kheo, nhu la khong phong dat. Khong phong dat, nay cac Ty-kheo, đua đen ich loi lon.

TTC 3(Nhu so 1, chi the vao “nhu la bieng nhac”)….

TTC 4(Nhu so 2, chi the vao “nhu la tinh can tinh tan”)…

TTC 5(Nhu so 1, chi the vao “nhu la nhieu duc”)…

TTC 6(Nhu so 2, chi the vao “nhu la it duc”)…

TTC 7(Nhu so 1, chi the vao “nhu la khong biet đu”)…

TTC 8(Nhu so 2, chi the vao “nhu la biet đu”)…

TTC 9(Nhu so 1, chi the vao “nhu la khong nhu ly tac y”)…

TTC 10(Nhu so 2, chi the vao “nhu la nhu ly tac y”)…

TTC 11(Nhu so 1, chi the vao “nhu la khong tinh giac”)…

TTC 12(Nhu so 2, chi the vao “nhu la tinh giac”)…

TTC 13(Nhu so 1, chi the vao “nhu la lam ban voi ac”)…

TTC 14(Nhu so 2, chi the vao “nhu la lam ban voi thien”)…

TTC 15Ta khong thay mot phap nao khac, nay cac Ty-kheo, co the đua đen bat loi lon, nay cac Ty-kheo, nhu la he luy voi cac phap bat thien, khong he luy voi cac phap thien. He luy voi cac phap bat thien, nay cac Ty-kheo, khong he luy voi cac phap thien, đua đen bat loi lon.

TTC 16Ta khong thay mot phap nao khac, nay cac Ty-kheo, đua đen loi ich lon, nay cac Ty-kheo, nhu la he luy voi cac phap thien, khong he luy voi cac phap bat thien. He luy cac phap thien, nay cac Ty-kheo, khong he luy voi cac phap bat thien đua đen loi ich lon.
