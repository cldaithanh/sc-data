47.44. Niem

Vi-n 1 Tai Savatthi …

Vi-n 2 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, hay tru chanh niem. Ðay la loi giao gioi cua Ta cho cac Ong.

Vi-n 3 Va nay cac Ty-kheo, the nao la Ty-kheo chanh niem?

O đay, nay cac Ty-kheo, Ty-kheo tru, quan than tren than … tru, quan tho tren cac cam tho … tru, quan tam tren tam … tru, quan phap tren cac phap, nhiet tam, tinh giac, chanh niem, nhiep phuc tham uu o đoi. Nhu vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, la Ty-kheo chanh niem.

Vi-n 4 Nay cac Ty-kheo, Ty-kheo hay tru chanh niem. Ðay la loi giao gioi cua Ta cho cac Ong.
