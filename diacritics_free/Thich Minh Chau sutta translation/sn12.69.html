12.69. Nuoc Thuy Trieu Dang: Upayanti

Vi-n 1 Nhu vay toi nghe. Mot thoi The Ton tru o Savatthi, tai Jetavana, trong vuon ong Anathapindika.

Vi-n 2 Tai đay…

Vi-n 3 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, khi nao bien lon dang len thoi cac song lon dang len; khi nao cac song lon dang len thoi cac song nho dang len; khi nao cac song nho dang len thoi cac ho lon dang len; khi nao cac ho lon dang len thoi cac ho nho dang len.

Vi-n 4 Cung vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, khi nao vo minh dang len thoi cac hanh dang len; khi nao cac hanh dang len thoi thuc dang len; khi nao thuc dang len thoi danh sac dang len; khi nao danh sac dang len thoi sau xu dang len; khi nao sau xu dang len thoi xuc dang len; khi nao xuc dang len thoi tho dang len; khi nao tho dang len thoi ai dang len; khi nao ai dang len thoi thu dang len; khi nao thu dang len thoi huu dang len; khi nao huu dang len thoi sanh dang len; khi nao sanh dang len thoi gia chet dang len.

Vi-n 5 Nay cac Ty-kheo, bien lon khong dang len thoi song lon khong dang len; song lon khong dang len thoi song nho khong dang len; song nho khong dang len thoi ho lon khong dang len; ho lon khong dang len thoi ho nho khong dang len.

Vi-n 6 Cung vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, vo minh khong dang len thoi cac hanh khong dang len; cac hanh khong dang len thoi thuc khong dang len; thuc khong dang len thoi danh sac khong dang len; danh sac khong dang len thoi sau xu khong dang len; sau xu khong dang len thoi xuc khong dang len; xuc khong dang len thoi tho khong dang len; tho khong dang len thoi ai khong dang len; ai khong dang len thoi thu khong dang len; thu khong dang len thoi huu khong dang len; huu khong dang len thoi sanh khong dang len; sanh khong dang len thoi gia chet khong dang len.
