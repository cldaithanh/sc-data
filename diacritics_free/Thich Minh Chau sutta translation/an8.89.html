8.89. Buoc Toi

TTC 1—Ðoi voi Ty-kheo thanh tuu tam phap, nay cac Ty-kheo, neu chung Tang muon, chung Tang co the lam Yet-ma buoc toi. The nao la tam?

TTC 2Co gang đem lai thiet hai cho cac gia chu, co gang đem lai bat loi cho cac gia chu, lam ly gian giua cac gia chu, huy bang Phat, huy bang Phap, huy bang chung Tang, khong thuc hien loi hua đung phap cho cac gia chu.

Ðoi voi Ty-kheo thanh tuu tam phap, nay cac Ty-kheo, neu chung Tang muon, chung Tang co the lam Yet-ma buoc toi.

TTC 3Ðoi voi Ty-kheo thanh tuu tam phap, nay cac Ty-kheo, neu chung Tang muon, chung Tang co the giai toa buoc toi Yet-ma. The nao la tam?

TTC 4Khong co gang đem lai thiet hai cho cac gia chu, khong co gang đem lai bat loi cho cac gia chu, khong co gang ly gian giua cac gia chu, khong huy bang Phat, khong huy bang Phap, khong huy bang chung Tang, thuc hien loi hua đung Phap cho cac gia chu.

Ðoi voi Ty-kheo thanh tuu tam phap, nay cac Ty-kheo, neu chung Tang muon, chung Tang co the giai toa buoc toi Yet-ma.
