6.15. Khong Hoi Han

TTC 1O noi đay, Ton gia Sariputta goi cac Ty-kheo:

—Nay chu Hien, Ty-kheo neu song nep song nhu vay, nhu vay, do nep song nhu vay, khi lam chung co hoi han, khi menh chung co hoi han. Va nhu the nao, nay cac Hien gia, Ty-kheo song nep song nhu vay, nhu vay, khi lam chung co hoi han, khi menh chung co hoi han?

TTC 2… (Giong nhu kinh so 14, chi khac la thay the khong hien thien bang co hoi han, thay the co hien thien bang khong hoi han)…

Bo kinh nay đuoc Hoa thuong Thich Minh Chau dich tu tang Pali sang Viet ngu nam 1976–1977, va đuoc Vien Phat Hoc Van Hanh, Sai Gon, an hanh nam 1980–1981. Trong chuong trinh phien dich va an hanh Ðai tang kinh Viet Nam, bo kinh đuoc hieu đinh thanh 4 tap va tai ban nam 1996, qua so thu tu 21, 22, 23, va 24.
