• Chuong 22: Tuong Ung Uan
  • V: Pham Kien: Ditthivagga

22.150. Noi: Ajjhatta

Vi-n 1-2 O Savatthi…

Vi-n 3 —Do co cai gi, nay cac Ty-kheo, do chap thu cai gi, noi lac, kho khoi len?

Vi-n 4 —Bach The Ton, đoi voi chung con, cac phap lay The Ton lam can ban…

Vi-n 5 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, do co sac, do chap thu sac, noi lac, kho khoi len.

Vi-n 6-8 … tho… tuong… cac hanh…

Vi-n 9 Do co thuc, do chap thu thuc, noi lac, kho khoi len.

Vi-n 10 Cac ong nghi the nao, nay cac Ty-kheo, sac la thuong hay vo thuong?

—Vo thuong, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong la kho hay lac?

—La kho, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong, kho, chiu su bien hoai, neu khong chap thu cai ay, noi lac, kho co the khoi len khong?

—Thua khong, bach The Ton.

Vi-n 11-13 … Tho… Tuong… Cac hanh…

Vi-n 14 —Thuc la thuong hay vo thuong?

—Vo thuong, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong la kho hay lac?

—La kho, bach The Ton.

—Cai gi vo thuong, kho, chiu su bien hoai, neu khong chap thu cai ay, thoi noi lac, kho co the sanh khoi khong?

—Thua khong, bach The Ton.

Vi-n 15 Do thay vay… “… khong con tro lui trang thai nay nua”.
