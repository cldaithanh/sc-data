46.10. Sanh (2)

Vi-n 1 …

Vi-n 2 —Bay giac chi nay, nay cac Ty-kheo, chua khoi len, neu đuoc tu tap, lam cho sung man, co the khoi len. Nhung khong the khong co gioi luat cua bac Thien The. The nao la bay?

Vi-n 3 Niem giac chi … xa giac chi. Bay giac chi nay, nay cac Ty-kheo, chua khoi len, neu đuoc tu tap, đuoc lam cho sung man, co the khoi len. Nhung khong the khong co gioi luat cua bac Thien The.
