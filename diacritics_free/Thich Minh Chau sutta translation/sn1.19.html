1.19. Am Tranh

Ngai khong co am tranh,
Ngai khong co to am,
Ngai khong co day giang,
Ngai thoat khoi he phuoc.

(The Ton):

Ta khong co am tranh,
Ta khong co to am,
Ta khong co day giang,
Ta thoat khoi he phuoc.

(Vi Thien):

Con noi am la gi?
Noi to am la gi?
Noi day giang la gi?
Noi he phuoc la gi?

(The Ton):

Ong noi am la me,
Noi to am la vo,
Noi day giang la con,
Noi he phuoc la ai.

(Vi Thien):

Lanh thay, Ngai khong am!
Lanh thay, khong to am!
Lanh thay, khong day giang!
Lanh thay, Ngai thoat phuoc!
