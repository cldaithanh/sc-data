2.42. Dhammapala

BJT 203“Hien nhien, vi ty khuu tre tuoi, no luc theo loi day cua đuc Phat, la nguoi tinh thuc giua nhung ke ngu say; mang song cua vi ay khong phai rong khong.

BJT 204Boi vay, bac thong minh, trong khi ghi nho loi day cua chu Phat, nen ra suc ve đuc tin, ve gioi, ve niem hoan hy, va ve viec nhan thuc Giao Phap.”

Đai đuc truong lao Dhammapala đa noi nhung loi ke nhu the.

Ke ngon cua truong lao Dhammapala.
