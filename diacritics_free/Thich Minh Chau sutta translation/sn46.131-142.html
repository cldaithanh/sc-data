46.131

Vi-n 1 …

Vi-n 2 —Vi nhu, nay cac Ty-kheo, song Hang thien ve phuong Ðong, huong ve phuong Ðong, xuoi ve phuong Ðong. Cung vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, Ty-kheo tu tap bay giac chi, thien ve Niet-ban, huong ve Niet-ban, xuoi ve Niet-ban.

Vi-n 3 O đay, nay cac Ty-kheo, Ty-kheo tu tap niem giac chi … tu tap xa giac chi voi muc đich nhiep phuc tham, voi muc đich nhiep phuc san, voi muc đich nhiep phuc si. Cung vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, Ty-kheo … xuoi ve Niet-ban.
