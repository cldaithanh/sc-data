48.68. Cay (2)

Vi-n 1 …

Vi-n 2 —Vi nhu, nay cac Ty-kheo, pham co nhung cay cua chu Thien coi Ba muoi ba, cay Parichattaka đuoc xem la toi thuong. Cung vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, pham co nhung phap thuoc phan giac ngo, tue can đuoc xem la toi thuong, tuc la ve giac ngo.

Vi-n 3 Va nay cac Ty-kheo, the nao la nhung phap thuoc phan giac ngo? Tin can, nay cac Ty-kheo, la phap thuoc phan giac ngo … Tue can la phap thuoc phan giac ngo, vi phap ay đua đen giac ngo. Vi nhu, nay cac Ty-kheo, pham co nhung cay gi cua chu Thien coi Ba muoi ba, cay Paaricchattaka đuoc xem la toi thuong. Cung vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, pham co nhung phap thuoc phan giac ngo, tue can đuoc xem la toi thuong, tuc la ve giac ngo.
