5.198. Loi Noi

TTC 1—Thanh tuu nam chi phan, nay cac Ty-kheo, cac loi la thien thuyet, khong phai ac thuyet, khong co pham toi va khong bi nhung nguoi co tri chi trich. The nao la nam?

TTC 2Noi đung thoi, noi đung su that, noi loi nhu hoa, noi loi lien he đen loi ich, noi voi loi tu tam.

Thanh tuu nam chi phan, nay cac Ty-kheo, cac loi la thien thuyet, khong phai ac thuyet, khong co pham toi va khong bi nhung nguoi co tri chi trich.
