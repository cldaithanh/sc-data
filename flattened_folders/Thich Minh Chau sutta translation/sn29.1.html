• Chuong 29: Tuong Ung Loai Rong

29.1. Chung Loai

Vi-n 1-2. Nhan duyen o Savatthi …

Vi-n 3 —Nay cac Ty-kheo co bon loai Naga (rong, ran) ve sanh chung. The nao la bon? Loai Naga tu trung sanh, loai Naga tu thai sanh, loai Naga tu am uot sanh, loai Naga hoa sanh. Nay cac Ty-kheo, đay la bon loai Naga theo sanh chung.
