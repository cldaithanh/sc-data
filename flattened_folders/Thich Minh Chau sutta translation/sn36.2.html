36.2. An Lac

S.iv,204Vi-n 1-2. …

Vi-n 3 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, co ba tho nay. The nao la ba? Lac tho, kho tho, bat kho bat lac tho. Ðay, nay cac Ty-kheo, la ba tho.

Vi-n 1 Lac tho hay kho tho,
Cung bat kho bat lac,
Noi tho va ngoai tho,
Pham co cam tho gi.

Vi-n 2 Biet đuoc: “Ðay la kho”,
Hu nguy va bien hoai,
Thay xuc, xuc bien diet,
O đay, song ly tham.
