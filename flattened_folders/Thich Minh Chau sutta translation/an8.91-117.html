8.91–117. Mot So Nu Cu Si

Bojjha, Sirima, Paduma, Sudhana, Manuja, Uttara, Mutta, Khema, Soma Rupi, Cundi Bimbi, Sumana, Mallika, Tissa, me cua Tissa, Sona, me cua Cunda Sona, Kana, me cua Kana, Uttara, me cua Nanda, Visakha, me cua Migara, nu cu si Khujjuttara va Samavati, Suppavasa, nguoi thieu nu Koliya, nu cu si Suppiya, nu gia chu me cua Nakula.

Bo kinh nay đuoc Hoa thuong Thich Minh Chau dich tu tang Pali sang Viet ngu nam 1976–1977, va đuoc Vien Phat Hoc Van Hanh, Sai Gon, an hanh nam 1980–1981. Trong chuong trinh phien dich va an hanh Ðai tang kinh Viet Nam, bo kinh đuoc hieu đinh thanh 4 tap va tai ban nam 1996, qua so thu tu 21, 22, 23, va 24.
