3. Ba Muoi Hai Phan

Trong than nay co: toc, long, mong, rang, da, thit, gan, xuong, tuy, than, tim, gan, hoanh cach mo, la lach, phoi, ruot, mang ruot, bung, phan, mat, đam, mu, mau, mo hoi, mo, nuoc mat, mo da, nuoc mieng, nuoc mu, chat nhon o khop, nuoc tieu va nao trong đau.

Translated by Thich Minh Chau.

Prepared for SuttaCentral by Ven. Vimala.
