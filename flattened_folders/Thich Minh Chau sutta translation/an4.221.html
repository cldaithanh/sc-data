4.221. Dieu Hanh

TTC 1—Nay cac Ty-kheo, co bon loi ac hanh nay. The nao la bon?

Noi lao, noi hai luoi, noi loi tho ac, noi loi phu phiem.

Nay cac Ty-kheo, co bon loi ac hanh nay.

TTC 2Nay cac Ty-kheo, co bon loi thien hanh nay. The nao la bon?

Noi that, khong noi hai luoi, noi loi nhu nhuyen, noi loi thong tue.

Nay cac Ty-kheo, co bon loi thien hanh nay.
