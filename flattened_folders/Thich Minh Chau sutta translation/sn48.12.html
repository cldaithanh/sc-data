48.12. Tom Tat (1)

Vi-n 1 …

Vi-n 2 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, co nam can nay. The nao la nam? Tin can … tue can. Nhung phap nay, nay cac Ty-kheo, la nam can.

Vi-n 3 Voi su toan dien (samatta), nay cac Ty-kheo, voi su vien man nam can nay, la bac A-la-han. Yeu nhe hon la bac Bat hoan. Yeu nhe hon la bac Nhat lai. Yeu nhe hon la bac Du luu. Yeu nhe hon nua la bac Tuy phap hanh. Yeu nhe hon nua la bac Tuy tin hanh.
