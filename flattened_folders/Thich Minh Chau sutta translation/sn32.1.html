• Chuong 32: Tuong Ung Than May

32.1. Chung Loai

Vi-n 1-2. Tru o Savatthi …

Vi-n 3 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, Ta se thuyet cho cac Ong ve chu Thien thuoc loai than may. Hay lang nghe.

Vi-n 4 —Nay cac Ty-kheo, the nao la chu Thien thuoc loai than may? Co chu Thien, nay cac Ty-kheo, thuoc loai may lanh. Co chu Thien, nay cac Ty-kheo, thuoc loai may nong. Co chu Thien, nay cac Ty-kheo, thuoc loai may sam. Co chu Thien, nay cac Ty-kheo, thuoc loai may gio. Co chu Thien, nay cac Ty-kheo, thuoc loai may mua.

Vi-n 5 Nhu vay, nay cac Ty-kheo, ho đuoc goi la chu Thien thuoc loai than may.
