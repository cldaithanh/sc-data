5.160. Kho Tru Khu

TTC 1—Nam phap nay khoi len, nay cac Ty-kheo, that kho tru khu. The nao la nam?

TTC 2Tham khoi len, that kho tru khu; san khoi len, that kho tru khu; si khoi len, that kho tru khu; ham noi khoi len, that kho tru khu; tam lang thang that kho tru khu.

Nam phap nay đa khoi len, nay cac Ty-kheo, that kho tru khu.

Bo kinh nay đuoc Hoa thuong Thich Minh Chau dich tu tang Pali sang Viet ngu nam 1976–1977, va đuoc Vien Phat Hoc Van Hanh, Sai Gon, an hanh nam 1980–1981. Trong chuong trinh phien dich va an hanh Ðai tang kinh Viet Nam, bo kinh đuoc hieu đinh thanh 4 tap va tai ban nam 1996, qua so thu tu 21, 22, 23, va 24.
